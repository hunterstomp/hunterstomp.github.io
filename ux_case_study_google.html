Google Shopping Prospect Journey – T-Mobile Integration

Project Summary

Client: T-Mobile via Google Shopping

Role: UX Architect, Journey Designer

Team: Marketing, Engineering, Retail Experience

Focus: Seamless e-commerce conversion optimization from Google search to in-store pickup

Problem

Prospects were arriving on product pages with intent to purchase, but friction in configuration, checkout, and confirmation was causing drop-offs.

Goal

Optimize the Google Shopping product flow to reduce re-entry steps and leverage store availability APIs to streamline decision-making and boost confidence.

Research & Discovery

  • Mapped prospect behavior from keyword search to PDP to retail pickup【19†Google Shopping】
  • Analyzed conversion friction: reconfiguring specs, unclear financing, confirmation gaps
  • Identified WOW moments tied to stock visibility and location awareness
“I was expecting more of an order number and confirmation here…”
“Great, it’s still configured the way I like. I want it.”

UX Approach

  • Preserved configuration from Google → T-Mobile PDP
  • Enabled location-aware inventory checks and map directions
  • Improved microcopy on soft confirmation screens
