ASU IoY Journey Maps: Hypertension & Diabetes

Summary Challenges Objectives Gallery Solutions Outcomes Future Video

Project Summary

This project focused on creating clear and actionable UX journey maps for patients managing chronic conditions, specifically hypertension and diabetes, using the Internet of You (IoY) platform at Arizona State University.

UX Challenges

  • Lack of clear patient guidance for chronic disease management.
  • Difficulty tracking symptoms and vital signs effectively.
  • Patients feeling overwhelmed with data inputs and medical terminology.

Design Objectives

  • Simplify the patient journey through intuitive UI and actionable guidance.
  • Clearly visualize data tracking and symptom management for ease of use.
  • Ensure accessibility for a diverse patient demographic.

Design Gallery

UX Solutions

  • Developed distinct, easy-to-follow journey maps for hypertension and diabetes.
  • Designed user-friendly data entry forms with clear labeling and interactive feedback.
  • Implemented visual symptom trackers and progress indicators.

Impact & Outcomes

  • Improved patient adherence to chronic disease management routines.
  • Positive user feedback on clarity and ease-of-use.
  • Reduced patient anxiety regarding health data tracking.

Future Enhancements

  • Integrate predictive analytics to proactively alert patients of potential health issues.
  • Expand journey maps to cover additional chronic conditions.
  • Personalize content dynamically based on user interactions and health data.
