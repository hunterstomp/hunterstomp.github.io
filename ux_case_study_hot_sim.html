HOT SIM Activation – Preventing Billing Confusion

Project Summary

Client: T-Mobile (Internal CX Initiative)

Role: UX Troubleshooter, Notification Designer

Platform: iOS App, Email, In-Box SIM Delivery

Focus: Clarify and simplify dual-device activation to avoid confusion and billing complaints

Problem

Customers using T-Mobile's dual-SIM activation process were unintentionally activating both new and old phones, resulting in duplicate line charges and panic about billing statements.

Goal

Design a user-centric flow that communicates the 7-day overlap clearly, avoids surprises in billing, and guides users through new-device activation confidently.

Research & Discovery

  • Mapped entire lifecycle from order placement to phone swap and SIM pairing【20†HOT SIM Journey Map】
  • Reviewed user logs and support transcripts: most complaints tied to unclear “2 lines” billing alert
  • Identified false assumptions about SIM insertion timing vs. billing
“Why am I being billed for a 2nd line?!”
“I expect that I will have to swap SIM cards”

UX Approach

  • Created a new onboarding alert inside the T-Mobile app titled: “You’re Almost Done – Here’s What Happens Next”
  • Added SMS and email reminders about the 7-day transition and temporary dual-activation
  • Visualized device roles: New = Master, Old = Duplicate

Outcomes

Testimonials

What I’d Improve

  • Add animation to onboarding to show new/old phone roles
  • Explore tooltips on billing page to explain overlapping charges
  • Let users manually opt into “duplicate line” mode for side-by-side devices
